About FoodLoverTV

At Food Lovers TV, our vision is to bring you engaging videos that showcase the most fascinating stories from the world of food. Join me on Gourmet On The Road as I travel across India and beyond to savour time-honoured culinary traditions, explore restaurants that have evolved from mere eateries to formidable institutions and document interesting food stories. Food has the magical ability to bring people together, no matter what cultures backgrounds or countries we may come from. I hope you will savour this exciting culinary journey with us, do subscribe to the channel.
